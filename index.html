I am undergraduate at the University of Chicago, double majoring in Physics and Astrophysics. My prior research experiences include work on black hole X-ray binaries (under Vicky Kalogera and Christopher Berry), exo-planet dynamics (under Daniel Fabrycky) and supernova remnants (under Vikram Dwarkadas and Kari Frank).

Moving forward, I am particularly interested in the growing field of time-domain astrophysics, as well as continuing my work with UChicago Taking the Next Step and the SPS board to advance science communication and representation in STEM fields.
