I am an undergraduate at the University of Chicago, majoring in Physics and Astrophysics. My prior research experiences include studies of:
  • Low-mass black hole X-ray binaries with Vicky Kalogera and Christopher Berry, supported by the NSF Northwestern CIERA REU program
  • Exoplanet dynamics with Daniel Fabrycky at the University of Chicago, supported by the College Research Fellows (Hoeft) Award
  • Supernova remnants with Vikram Dwarkadas of the University of Chicago and Kari Frank of Northwestern University
Moving forward, I am particularly interested in the growing field of time-domain astrophysics, as well as continuing my work with UChicago Taking the Next Step and the Society of Physics Students (SPS) board to advance science communication and representation in STEM fields.
